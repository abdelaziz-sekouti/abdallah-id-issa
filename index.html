Biography

Abdellah Id'issa was born in Agadir, Morocco, in the heart of the country's southern coast. From a young age, he was fascinated by the vast landscapes stretching from the Atlantic Ocean to the Sahara Desert, from the snow-capped Atlas Mountains to the ancient medinas of Marrakech and Fez.

A retired schoolteacher and storyteller, Abdellah spent decades gathering tales from remote villages, learning forgotten routes through mountains and deserts, and building relationships with nomads and mountain communities. His dream was always to walk the full perimeter of his country—up to Tangier, across to the eastern border near Oujda, down through the dramatic Dades Gorge at Gargarat, and back to his beloved Agadir.

The Complete Morocco Circuit

Agadir (Start) — He left his hometown with nothing but a backpack, his camera, and a map drawn from 30 years of walking.
Ouarzazate → Marrakech — Through the ancient kasbahs and the bustling souks of the Red City, absorbing centuries of history.
Chefchaouen — The blue pearl where he spent days listening to stories in the plaza.
Fez → Oujda — The spiritual heart of Morocco and the eastern frontier.
Errachidia → Rissani → Tinghir — The edge of the Sahara where dunes meet mountains.
Gargarat (Dades Gorge) — The dramatic canyon where he completed the hardest stretch, earning his title "The Record of Morocco."
Merzouga → Zagora → Taznakht → Agadir — The final loop home, covering over 2,500 km on foot.

Self-funded journey

Abdellah didn't just walk across Morocco—he became a bridge between cultures. He documented the lives of Berber mountain communities, recorded the oral histories of Saharian nomads, and helped repair wells and schools in forgotten villages. His journey was named "The Record of Morocco" by local authorities.

Legacy

Today Abdellah shares his journey through storytelling, photography, and community projects. He has inspired dozens of young Moroccans to explore their own country on foot, and his route has become a symbol of national pride—a walking map that connects every corner of the Kingdom.
